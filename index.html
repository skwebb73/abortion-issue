THE ABORTION ISSUE

The U.S. Supreme Court overturned Roe v. Wade on June 24, 2022.

This set off a wave of protests across the nation and brought to the foreground long standing debates between pro-life and pro-choice groups.

The Daily Tar Heel has published a special edition of its weekly newspaper to highlight the impacts of these decisions on people with uteruses both in Orange County and at UNC.

All of the content for this issue was created by female and nonbinary members of the newsroom.
